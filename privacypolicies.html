PRIVACY POLICIES

IJFER is committed to protecting the privacy of the visitors, users and Authors of the websites. We have adopted the following guidelines to ensure the privacy of users. IJFER has all the right to change these guidelines as and when required. Any changes in these guidelines will be posted in website. Users might not get any communication in such change of policies.

The personal information of the website users we collects will depend on how user using the IJFER Site. IJFER collect information certain non-personally identifiable information, such as the type of operating system, type of browser and the domain name of your Internet Service Provider you using. Personally identifiable information if required we may collect through contact information you send to us. IJFER does not share, rent or sell personal information of users with other people or organization except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission. IJFER use the personal information in ways that are compatible with the purposes for which it was intended to be used to enable your use of the Site; to quick respond to your inquiries; for system administration, troubleshooting purposes, customer support to improve the design of the Site and journal; to enable us to enforce our Terms and Conditions of Use.

IJFER believe it is necessary to share information in order to prevent, investigate or take action regarding , suspected fraud, illegal activities, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, or as otherwise required by law. IJFER contains external links to other websites and organizations and we do not control the use of information or data collection on external websites. We encourage you to read their terms, conditions and privacy policies and. This privacy policy governs only the IJFER .

SECURITY

IJFER committed to ensuring that your Personal information is safe. In order to prevent disclosure or unauthorized access, we have use suitable electronic, physical and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the Personal information we collect.

  • We control access of unauthorized persons so that only people who need to access the Personal Data can access.
  • All the Personal Data is stored in a secure computing environment protected by high security systems and firewalls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • IJFER use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol with 256-bit RSA encryption. This means that all the information sent between your computer and our secure computer is encrypted so that no one can read it in transit.
